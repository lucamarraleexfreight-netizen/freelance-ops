# Acme Cloud — Pricing & Billing

## Plans

Acme Cloud offers three plans:

- **Starter** — $19/user/month, billed annually. Includes 5 projects, 10 GB
  storage, and email support.
- **Team** — $49/user/month, billed annually. Unlimited projects, 100 GB
  storage, priority support, and SSO.
- **Enterprise** — custom pricing. Adds dedicated infrastructure, a named
  customer success manager, and a 99.95% uptime SLA.

Monthly (non-annual) billing is available on Starter and Team at a 20% premium.

## Refunds

Annual plans can be cancelled within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.
After 30 days, annual plans are non-refundable but remain active until the end
of the billing period. Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime and are not
prorated.

## Taxes

Prices exclude applicable sales tax and VAT, which are added at checkout based
on the billing address.
