# Acme Cloud — Onboarding Guide

## Creating your workspace

After signing up, you'll be prompted to name your workspace and invite team
members. Each workspace is isolated; data is never shared between workspaces.
The person who creates the workspace becomes the initial admin.

## Importing data

You can import existing data from CSV or via the REST API. CSV imports support
up to 50,000 rows per file. For larger migrations, use the bulk import endpoint,
which accepts up to 5 million rows per job and processes them asynchronously.

## Inviting your team

Admins invite members by email from Settings → Members. Invited users receive a
link valid for 7 days. You can assign each member a role (Admin, Editor, or
Viewer) at invite time or change it later.

## First project

Create your first project from the dashboard's "New Project" button. Projects
can be duplicated from templates. Starter is limited to 5 projects; Team and
Enterprise are unlimited.

## Getting help

New Team and Enterprise customers get a free 30-minute onboarding call. Book it
from the welcome banner during your first week.
