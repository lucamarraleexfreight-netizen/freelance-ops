# Acme Cloud — Support & SLAs

## Support channels

- **Starter**: email support, responses within 2 business days.
- **Team**: priority email and in-app chat, responses within 4 business hours.
- **Enterprise**: 24/7 phone, chat, and a dedicated Slack channel, with a
  15-minute response target for critical issues.

## Severity levels

- **Sev 1 (critical)** — production down or data loss. Enterprise response
  target: 15 minutes, 24/7.
- **Sev 2 (major)** — major feature broken, no workaround. Response target: 4
  business hours.
- **Sev 3 (minor)** — minor issue or question. Response target: 2 business days.

## Uptime SLA

The Enterprise plan includes a 99.95% monthly uptime SLA. If uptime falls below
that, customers receive service credits: 10% of monthly fees for 99.0–99.95%,
and 25% below 99.0%. Credits must be requested within 30 days.

## Maintenance windows

Scheduled maintenance occurs on the first Sunday of each month, 02:00–04:00 UTC.
Customers are notified at least 7 days in advance.
